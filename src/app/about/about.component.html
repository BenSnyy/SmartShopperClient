About Smart Shopper

Our Mission

To save you time and money by providing an on the spot ability to see all the groceries you have at home. Also to be more green by helping you waste less food that expired and less gas driving back to the store for something your forgot.

Why Smart Shopper?

Have you ever been at the grocery store and couldn't remember if you had something at home or not? Or know you have it, but did it expire? We all have! So you could buy it, potentially wasting money and food, or risk not buying it and then not have something you need. With Smart Shopper, you can log in on your smart phone and see everything you have at home and when it expires! Never waste money or food again! With Smart Shopper.
